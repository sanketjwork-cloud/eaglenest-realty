Overview

Luxury Apartments in Lokhandwala, Andheri (W)

Purva Estrella is a thoughtfully planned gated development where every element is placed with intent. From generous open spaces and landscaped greens to curated amenities and everyday conveniences, the community is designed to support living well, naturally and effortlessly.

Set within the heart of Andheri West, Purva Estrella creates a sense of openness rarely found in the city. Expansive greens, generous breathing spaces, and thoughtful planning come together to shape a calmer way of living — inviting you to indulge in your wellbeing, naturally.

From open spaces to curated amenities, every element is designed to support a calmer, more considered way of living.
