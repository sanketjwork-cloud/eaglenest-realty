Overview

Welcome to Ajmera Vann Versova

Vann by Ajmera is a newly launched prestigious residential enclave in Versova, Mumbai. Inspired by Singapore-style landscapes and surrounded by 60% lush green open spaces, it presents 61 exclusive boutique residences.

Crafted according to Vaastu Shastra for a serene living experience, these 3 BHK and 4 BHK residences offer panoramic mangrove views from their expansive private decks. Thoughtfully curated amenities span the ground floor, podium, and rooftop.

Situated in one of Mumbai's most pristine suburbs, Vann by Ajmera is minutes away from prominent schools, colleges, hospitals, corporate parks, and shopping destinations — a truly complete address.
